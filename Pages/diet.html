Healthy Lifestyle for Students

A healthy lifestyle helps students stay active, focused, and stress-free. This page shows a simple and affordable diet plan with daily activities.

Simple & Affordable Diet Plan

Meal Food Items Benefits
Breakfast Egg, Paratha / Bread, Milk Provides energy
Mid-Morning Apple / Banana Improves digestion
Lunch Rice, Daal, Yogurt Builds strength
Evening Snack Roasted chana, Tea Healthy and low cost
Dinner Roti, Sabzi, Salad Light and nutritious

Healthy Activities for Students

  • 30 minutes walk or exercise daily
  • Drink 6–8 glasses of water
  • Avoid junk food
  • Take short breaks during study
  • Sleep on time
  • Play some mindfull games

Simple Daily Routine

  1. Wake up early
  2. Morning walk or exercise
  3. Healthy breakfast
  4. Study or attend classes
  5. Evening relaxation
  6. Dinner before 9 PM
  7. Sleep for 7–8 hours
