Introduction

A healthy lifestyle is essential for students to maintain physical fitness, mental well being, and academic performance. This website provides useful guidance on healthy eating habits, exercise routines, and balanced lifestyles. A healthy lifestyle is the foundation of a successful academic journey. Students often face stress due to studies, exams, and daily responsibilities, which can affect their health if ignored. Taking care of the body and mind is extremely important for better performance in studies and personal life.

This website guides students toward better eating habits, quality sleep, regular exercise and positive thinking. Our goal is to encourage students to live a balanced life where health, happiness, and success go hand in hand.

About

We are students of BS Artificial Intelligence at Institute of Space Technology (IST) creating this website is part of our academic/Final year project. The purpose of this project is to promote a healthy lifestyle among students by providing informative content on nutrition, physical activity, mental health, and daily routines. Through this initiative, we aim to create awareness about healthy habits and apply our web development skills to build a useful and educational platform for the student community.
